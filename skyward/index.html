microgravitational skies

微小重力無限天空


Endless skies in all six directions.

Gravity is but a suggestion whispered on the breeze, drowned out by the gentle roar of the midday sun.

A ferretsnake and a griffin have a chat. (caz)

Skyward is a hard science fiction worldbuilding project revolving around the lives of aliens living in a world of weightless skies called the Atmosphere. Within this ancient freefall megastructure are vast expanses of air containing oceans of clouds, bioengineered forests crawling with life, and a growing cluster of jet age civilizations, all bathed in biomechanical starlight.
