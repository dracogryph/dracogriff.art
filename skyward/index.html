SKYWARD

microgravitational skies


Skyward is a hard science fiction worldbuilding project revolving around the lives of aliens living in a world of weightless skies called the Atmosphere. Within this ancient freefall megastructure are vast expanses of air containing oceans of clouds, bioengineered forests crawling with life, and a growing cluster of jet age civilizations, lit by the light of biomechanical stars.
